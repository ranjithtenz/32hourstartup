Who all should come?
Anyone who wants to build a product - students, geeks, entrepreneurs, designers, VCs.

Yes, there will be WiFi blanketing the venue, and high speed connectivity is available to all participants at the event. Bring your own laptops!
Do I have to bring my own Laptop?
Yes, you will have to. We'll ensure a good supply of power strips :)
