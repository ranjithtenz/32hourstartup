What is it?

32 Hour Startup is an event that helps take your idea to launch within 32 hours. Turn up on Saturday morning, and have a product to demo by Sunday evening!

What are we supposed to do?

We provide you with awesome people to network with, a serene environment, free wifi and lots of caffeine.

  • Find an idea, build a Minimum Viable Product (MVP)
  • Join a team of people building something you think is cool
  • Pitch your idea to other people, and build it along with them
  • Pick an idea sponsored by an organization and implement it!

There will be a demo session where you can demo your stuf to all the people assembled - and gather valuable feedback and prizes!

When is it?

The event starts at Saturday, 5th February at 10:00 AM, and runs on till Demo time at Sunday, 6th February at 06:00 PM. Various short talks will be held scattered throughout the two days - stay tuned to this space for notifications.

How does it work?

  1. People pool their ideas on the ideas page.
  2. A gathering of startups, mentors, coders, designers and product researchers assemble on 5th morning
  3. Every one pitches their product ideas to the crowd (1 min per product)
  4. Teams of people decide to work on what they like and start building
  5. Mentors who can help are assigned to teams to get them started (as needed)
  6. The teams brainstorm, research, prototype and build a minimum viable product (MVP) for the next 30+ hours
  7. Chaos ensures and products are born
  8. At the end of the time, every one demos their products
  9. People vote for the ones they like most and provide feedback on all
  10. We/Teams decide what to do next and go back and get some sleep
